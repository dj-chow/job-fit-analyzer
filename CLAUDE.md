# Job Fit Analyzer - Project Instructions

## Writing Style (applies to ALL content in this repo)

- No em dashes. Use commas, periods, or "but" / "and" / "however" instead.
- Write like a human, not like AI. Short sentences. Conversational. Direct.
- Avoid AI-tell phrases: "leverage," "utilize," "in terms of," "it's worth noting," "comprehensive," "robust."
- Target a readability score of 4-5 on the Automated Readability Index.
- Use simple words over complex ones. "Use" not "utilize." "Show" not "demonstrate." "Help" not "facilitate."
- Vary sentence length. Mix short punchy sentences with slightly longer ones.
- When in doubt, read it out loud. If it sounds like a press release, rewrite it.
